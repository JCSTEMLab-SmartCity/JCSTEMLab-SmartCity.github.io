Wireless Communication and Networking

Advancing wireless communication technologies through innovative signal processing, secure communication protocols, and low-power networking solutions. Our research enables robust, efficient wireless systems for next-generation applications.

Secure and Robust Wireless Communication

Signal Processing and Security

  • Advanced signal processing techniques for interference mitigation and channel optimization.
  • Physical layer security methods for wireless communications.
  • Robust communication protocols resistant to jamming and eavesdropping.
  • Adaptive modulation and coding schemes for varying channel conditions.

Low-Power Communication Systems

  • Energy-efficient communication protocols for IoT and sensor networks.
  • Ultra-low power wireless design for battery-constrained devices.
  • Energy harvesting techniques for self-powered wireless systems.
  • Sleep scheduling and duty cycling optimization for network longevity.

Fast V2I Communications

  • High-speed vehicle-to-infrastructure communication protocols.
  • Real-time data exchange for autonomous driving applications.
  • Latency optimization in vehicular communication networks.
  • Bandwidth-efficient V2I communication schemes.
