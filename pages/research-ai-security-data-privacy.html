AI Security and Data Privacy

Protecting artificial intelligence models and sensitive data through advanced security mechanisms and privacy-preserving technologies. Our research ensures safe and secure AI deployment in smart city environments.

Model and Data Protection

AI Model IP Protection

  • Intellectual property protection for machine learning models and algorithms.
  • Model watermarking techniques to prevent unauthorized use and distribution.
  • Adversarial training methods to enhance model robustness against attacks.
  • Secure model deployment and inference in distributed environments.

Privacy-Preserving Data Transactions

  • Blockchain-based secure data sharing and transaction mechanisms.
  • Differential privacy techniques for protecting individual data records.
  • Federated learning systems that preserve data locality and privacy.
  • Homomorphic encryption for secure computation on encrypted data.
